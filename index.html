Welcome to AsianxADHD!

Wait...Asians can have ADHD?

Yup! We might don't get much representation, but we definitely exist!

So what's the point of this page?

You want one point? I'll give you several!

  • All the info about our ADHD Asians video meetings stored in one place
  • Collection of tips and lifehacks mentioned by me and attendees during meetings
  • Get TL;DR and TL;DW summaries of long articles and videos
  • A reason and practice area for my beginner web development skills
  • My personal ramble space so I don't fill up meetings and posts all the time

What's up with the funny font?

Many people with ADHD are also dyslexic, so I decided to use a font that makes it easier for dyslexic people to read. It's called OpenDyslexic and it's free! You can get it here!
